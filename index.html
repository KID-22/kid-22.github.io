I am a fourth-year Ph.D. student at Intelligent Information Retrieval Lab (IIR Lab) from the Gaoling School of Artificial Intelligence (GSAI), Renmin University of China (RUC), supervised by Prof. Jun Xu. From October 2024, I become a visiting Ph.D. student at NExT++ Research Centre, National University of Singapore (NUS), co-advised by Prof. Tat-Seng Chua and Prof. See-Kiong Ng. I feel grateful to collaborate closely with Prof. Wenjie Wang and Prof. Liang Pang. My research interests include Information Retrieval, Recommender System, and Large Language Model, particularly on bias-related challenges in these scenarios. My long-term goal is to build a trustworthy and responsible information access system. Currently, my primary focus is on leveraging the reasoning capabilities of LLMs to enhance search and recommendation tasks, while addressing the trustworthiness issues associated with LLM-generated content. I am always open to collaboration and discussions in these areas. If you are interested in, please feel free to reach out!

PS: I am currently on the job market (expected to graduate in June 2026) and actively seeking academic or industry opportunities. If you are interested in me, please feel free to contact me.
